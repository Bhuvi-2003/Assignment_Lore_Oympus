Major Characters in Lore Olympus

Hades

The ruler of the Underworld, complex with a soft side beneath his stoic demeanor.

Persephone

The goddess of spring, navigating love, power, and personal growth.

Zeus

The king of the gods, powerful yet indulgent, providing comic relief and tension.

Poseidon

The god of the sea, easygoing and fun-loving, bringing humor to the storyline.

Eros

The god of love, shaping romantic relationships and supporting Persephone.

Minor Characters in Lore Olympus

Hermes

The messenger god, known for his humor, speed, and energetic presence.

Apollo

The sun god, a darker, more antagonistic figure whose interactions with Persephone bring tension.

Artemis

Goddess of the hunt and a protective friend to Persephone, symbolizing loyalty.

Demeter

Persephone's protective mother, wary of the gods of Olympus, especially Hades.

Ares

God of war, a fierce but humorous character with complex relationships, especially with Aphrodite.
